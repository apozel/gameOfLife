Blokus is played on a 20x20 board. Everyone gets 21 shapes to place. Players play in a clockwise or counterclockwise rotation, with every player placing down 1 piece each turn. On the first turn players must place their pieces in the corners. Subsequent pieces must be played with only corner to corner contact with their own pieces. When a player cannot place a piece, he or she passes, and play continues. The game ends when no one can place a piece.
